## Related Topics Discovered

### FOREKNOWLEDGE OF GOD
1PE 1:20; REV 13:8; ACT 2:23 — Christ was foreordained/foreknown before creation, establishing the eternal basis of salvation

### PLAN OF SALVATION
From PROMPT.md scope: "which was given us in Christ Jesus before the world began" (2TI 1:9) — the plan predates creation

### BLOOD
The Nave's BLOOD topic includes the entire sacrificial system. Key principle: HEB 9:22 — "without shedding of blood is no remission" — applies across both testaments.

### PROPITIATION
ROM 3:25 uses hilasterion (G2435) — the same Greek word the LXX uses for the OT mercy seat (kapporeth, H3727). Christ IS the mercy seat toward which all OT atonement pointed.
