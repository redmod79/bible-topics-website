--- Genesis 15:6 ---
Hebrew Text: וְהֶאֱמִ֖ן בַּֽיהוָ֑ה וַיַּחְשְׁבֶ֥הָ לֹּ֖ו צְדָקָֽה׃

לֹּ֖ו           ל            Prep.+3ms                 to

KEY OBSERVATIONS:
1. הֶאֱמִ֖ן (he'emin) = Hiphil Perfect 3ms of אמן (aman)
   - Hiphil stem indicates causative: "he caused to be firm" = "he trusted/believed"
   - This is the root of emunah (H530, faithfulness/faith) and amen
   - Perfect tense = completed action: Abraham's belief was a definite act
2. וַיַּחְשְׁבֶ֥הָ (wayyachshebeh-a) = Qal Wayyiqtol 3ms + 3fs suffix of חשׁב (chashab)
   - The 3fs suffix refers back to his faith/believing (feminine concept)
   - "And He counted IT (his faith) to him"
   - The verb chashab means to reckon, account, impute — an accounting term
   - This is the OT basis for Paul's NT logizomai (G3049) in ROM 4
3. צְדָקָֽה (tsedaqah) = Noun, feminine singular absolute of צדקה
   - Means "righteousness, justice, rightness"
   - LXX translates this as dikaiosyne (G1343) — Paul's same word in ROM 4:3
