### Key Observation
REV 14:12 combines "keep the commandments of God" (entole G1785) with "the faith of Jesus" (pistis G4102). This is a distinctive Johannine combination:
- JHN 14:15: "If ye love me, keep my commandments" (entole)
- 1JN 5:3-4: "his commandments" + "faith" (nikao = overcoming through faith)
- REV 12:17: "keep the commandments of God, and have the testimony of Jesus Christ"
- REV 22:14: "Blessed are they that do his commandments"

The commandment-keeping + faith/testimony pattern spans all three bodies of Johannine literature.
