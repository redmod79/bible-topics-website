# Topics Research — The Man of Sin (2 Thess 2:3-4, 8-12)

## Question

Who is "the man of sin" / "son of perdition" / "that Wicked" / "the lawless one" in 2 Thess 2:3-12?

## Naves Topic Index (summary — full entries retrieved to `raw-data/naves-entries.txt`)

### ANTICHRIST

Nave's ANTICHRIST entry lists the figure under these biblical coordinates:

- **Warnings by Christ himself** — MAT 24:5, 23, 24, 26; MRK 13:6, 21, 22; LUK 21:8 ("many shall come in my name… I am Christ")
- **Paul's catalogue (the exact 2 Thess 2 pericope)** — 2 TH 2:3-12 (all twelve verses explicitly indexed under ANTICHRIST)
- **Johannine vocabulary** — 1 JN 2:18 ("antichrist shall come, even now are there many antichrists"); 1 JN 2:22 ("he is antichrist, that denieth the Father and the Son"); 1 JN 4:3 ("this is that spirit of antichrist"); 2 JN 1:7 ("This is a deceiver and an antichrist")
- **Destruction of the figure in Revelation** — REV 19:20 (beast + false prophet cast into lake of fire); REV 20:10, 15

Nave's ANTICHRIST therefore integrates three lexical streams the biblical text keeps distinct: Paul's *anthrōpos tēs anomias / huios tēs apōleias / ho anomos*; John's *antichristos*; and Revelation's *thērion*. The topic is a synthetic category; Paul himself does NOT use G500 antichristos.

### APOSTASY

**Gospels — Olivet-discourse apostasy prediction:**
- MAT 13:20-21 (stony-ground hearers "offended" in tribulation); MAT 24:9-12 ("many shall be offended… iniquity shall abound, love of many wax cold"); LUK 8:13 ("in time of temptation fall away")

**Epistles — late apostasy warning chain (bearing directly on 2 Thess 2:3):**

## Topic-Level Cross-Register Observation

The same pericope (2 Thess 2:3-12) is catalogued under FIVE distinct Nave's topics — ANTICHRIST, APOSTASY, BLASPHEMY, TEMPLE, WICKED, and MYSTERY — plus ghost-entries for WORSHIP (via sebasma at v.4) and SANCTUARY (via naos at v.4). This converging-multiplicity is itself an exegetical datum: a single figure is being described whose identification hangs on how these six topical registers are combined.
