    G3056 -- logos (word)¶

    • 272 total KJV occurrences, 55 unique translations
    • BLB count: 330
    • Top translations: word (115, 42.3%), words (41, 15.1%), saying (19, 7.0%), sayings (14, 5.1%)
    • Lexicon: masculine noun (n-m), transliteration "logos", from root meaning "something said (including the thought); by implication, a topic, reasoning, computation"
    • Key Johannine references: JHN 1:1,14 (christological title); 1JN 1:1,10; 2:5,7,14; 3:18; 5:7; REV 1:2,3,9,12; 3:8; 6:9; 12:11; 19:9,13; 20:4; 21:5; 22:6,10,18

    • BLB count: 30
    • Top translations: Lamb (24, 82.8%), a Lamb (2, 6.9%), Lamb's (2, 6.9%)

    G3875 -- parakletos (advocate/comforter)¶

    • No translations found in local Strong's database
    • No lexicon data found in local database
    • Data gap: The word is not in the local Strong's translation index. However, Greek parsing confirmed the Strong's number G3875 in JHN 14:16 (Parakleton, N-ASM) and 1JN 2:1 (Parakleton, N-ASM).
    • Known distribution: JHN 14:16,26; 15:26; 16:7; 1JN 2:1 (5 total NT uses, all Johannine)
    • Definition (from standard lexicons): an intercessor, consoler, advocate, comforter
    • Exclusively Johannine -- not used in Revelation or any other NT book
