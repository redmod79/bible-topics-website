    JOHN (the Apostle)¶

    Full Nave's Entry (from naves_db.py --topic "JOHN")¶

    1. The Baptist -- Prophecies concerning (ISA 40:3; MAL 4:5,6; LUK 1:11-17). Miraculous birth (LUK 1:11-20,57-65). Ministry (MAT 3:1-3; MRK 1:4; LUK 3:2,3; JHN 1:6-8). Testifies to Jesus (MAT 3:11,12; MRK 1:7,8; LUK 3:16,17; JHN 1:15,26-36; 3:23-36). Baptizes Jesus (MAT 3:13-16). Reproves Herod; imprisoned and beheaded (MAT 14:1-12; MRK 6:16-29).

    2. The Apostle -- Key biographical data: - Intimately associated with Jesus: JHN 13:23-26; 21:20 - Present at miracles: healing of Peter's mother-in-law (MAT 8:14,15), raising of Jairus's daughter (MRK 5:37), catches of fish (LUK 5:10; JHN 21:1-7), transfiguration (MAT 17:1; MRK 9:2; LUK 9:28) - Present in Gethsemane: MAT 26:37; MRK 14:33; LUK 22:39 - Intolerance of: MRK 9:38; LUK 9:49,50,54-56 - Civil ambitions: MAT 20:20-24; MRK 10:35-41 - Prepared the Passover meal: MAT 26:18,19; MRK 14:13-16; LUK 22:8-13 - Present at trial of Jesus before the high priest: JHN 18:15,16 - Present at the crucifixion: JHN 19:26,27 - Present at the gravesite: JHN 20:2-8 - Present when Jesus revealed himself at Galilee: JHN 21 - Present with Peter in the temple: ACT 3:1-11 - Lives in Jerusalem: ACT 1:13 - Entrusted with the care of Mary: JHN 19:26 - Imprisoned: ACT 4:1-19 - Sent to Samaria: ACT 8:14-17 - A pillar of the ekklesia: GAL 2:9 - Writes epistles: 1JN 1; 2JN 1; 3JN 1 - Writes apocalyptic vision from Patmos Island: REV 1:9 - Prophecy concerning: REV 10:11

    REVELATION¶

    Full Nave's Entry (from naves_db.py --topic "REVELATION")¶

    • God reveals himself to Moses: EXO 3:1-6,14; 6:1-3
    • The law is revealed: EXO 20-35; LEV 1-7
    • The pattern of the temple is revealed: 1CH 28:11-19
    • The sonship of Jesus is revealed: MAT 3:17; 16:17; 17:5
    • See also: INSPIRATION, PROPHECY, PROPHET, WORD OF GOD

    PROPHECY¶

    Full Nave's Entry Summary (from naves_db.py --topic "PROPHECY")¶

    • Inspired: ISA 28:22; LUK 1:70; 2TI 3:16; 2PE 1:21
    • "The word of the Lord came to" -- Elijah, Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Amos, Jonah, Haggai, Zechariah
    • Publicly proclaimed: JER 11:6
    • Exemplified in pantomime: EZK 4; 5:1-4; ACT 21:11
    • Proof of God's foreknowledge: ISA 43:9
    • Sure fulfillment: EZK 12:22-25,28; HAB 2:3; MAT 5:18; 24:35; ACT 13:27,29
    • Old Testament Messianic Passages quoted in the New Testament (extensive cross-reference list)

    REV 14:12 -- Concept Context¶
