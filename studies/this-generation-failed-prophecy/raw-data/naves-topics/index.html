    Raw Nave's Topic Data¶

    PROPHECY¶

    • Inspired: ISA 28:22; LUK 1:70; 2TI 3:16; 2PE 1:21
    • Sure fulfillment of: EZK 12:22-25,28; HAB 2:3; MAT 5:18; 24:35; ACT 13:27,29
    • Cessation of: LAM 2:9
    • Of apostasy: 1JN 2:18; Jude 1:17,18
    • False teachers: 2PE 2; 3
    • Tribulations of the righteous: REV 2:10
    • OLD TESTAMENT MESSIANIC PASSAGES QUOTED IN NT (extensive cross-reference list)
    • MISCELLANEOUS, FULFILLED (extensive list of specific fulfilled prophecies)

    TRANSFIGURATION¶

    • Of Moses: EXO 34:29-35
    • Of Jesus: MAT 17:2-9
    • Of Stephen: ACT 6:15

    KINGDOM OF HEAVEN¶

    • Compared to: man who sowed good seed (MAT 13:24-30,38-43; MRK 4:26-29), granule of mustard seed (MAT 13:31,32), leaven (MAT 13:33; LUK 13:21), treasure (MAT 13:44), pearl (MAT 13:45), net (MAT 13:47-50), king reckoning servants (MAT 18:23-35), householder (MAT 20:1-16), marriage feast (MAT 22:2-14), ten virgins (MAT 25:1-13), man traveling (MAT 25:14-30)
    • My kingdom is not of this world: JHN 18:36
    • Children of: MAT 18:3; 19:14; MRK 10:14; LUK 18:16
    • Rich people cannot enter: MAT 19:23,24; MRK 10:23-25; LUK 18:24,25,29,30
    • Keys of: MAT 16:19
    • Does not consist of meat and drink: ROM 14:17

    JERUSALEM (Destruction section)¶

    • Destruction of, foretold by Jesus: MAT 23:37,38; 24:15; MRK 13:14-23; LUK 13:35; 17:26-37; 19:41-44; 21:20-24
    • Prophecies against: ISA 3:1-8; JER 9:11; 19:6,15; 21:10; 26:9,11; DAN 9:2,27; MIC 1:1; 3:12
    • Of pestilence, famine, and war in: JER 34:2; EZK 5:12; JOL 3:2,3; AMO 2:5
    • Of the destruction of: JER 7:32-34; 26:18; 32:29,31,32; DAN 9:24-27

    JUDGMENT (THE GENERAL)¶

    • 1CH 16:33; JOB 14:17; 21:30; 31:13-15; PSA 9:7; 50:3-6; 96:13; 98:9; ECC 3:17; 11:9; 12:14; EZK 18:20-28; DAN 7:9,10; AMO 4:12; MAT 3:12; 7:22,23; 8:29; 10:15; 11:22; 12:36,37,41,42; 13:30,40-43,49,50; 16:27; 22:11-13; 23:14; 25:1-46; MRK 4:22; 8:38; 13:32; LUK 3:17; 10:10-14; 11:31,32; 12:2-5; 13:24-29; 19:12-26; 20:45-47; JHN 5:22; 12:48; ACT 2:19-21; 10:42; 17:31; 24:25; ROM 2:5-10,12-16; 14:10-12; 1CO 3:13; 4:5; 6:2; 2CO 5:10; 2TH 1:7,8; 2TI 4:1,8; HEB 6:2; 9:27; 10:27; 1PE 4:5,7; 2PE 2:4,9; 3:7,10-12; 1JN 4:17; Jude 1:6,14,15,24; REV 1:7; 6:15-17; 11:18; 20:11-15; 22:12

    VISION (Transfiguration section)¶

    • Peter, James, and John: Of the transfiguration of Jesus and the appearance of Moses and Elijah: MAT 17:1-9; LUK 9:28-36
    • Of Daniel: Of the four beasts: DAN 7; Of the Ancient of Days: DAN 7:9-27

    JESUS, THE CHRIST - KINGDOM OF (excerpted)¶

    • PROPHECIES CONCERNING UNIVERSALITY OF THE KINGDOM OF: GEN 12:3; 49:10; DEU 32:21; PSA 2:8; DAN 2:35,44; 7:13,14,18,22,27; MRK 13:10; REV 11:15; etc.
    • UNCLASSIFIED PROPHECIES CONCERNING THE KINGDOM OF: includes MAT 16:28; 2PE 1:11; DAN 7:9-14,27
    • SECULAR NOTIONS CONCERNING THE KINGDOM OF: MAT 18:1-3; 20:20-23; JHN 6:15; ACT 1:6,7
